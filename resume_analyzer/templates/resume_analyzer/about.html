About ATS Resume Checker

What is an ATS?

An Applicant Tracking System (ATS) is software used by employers to collect, sort, scan, and rank job applications. Over 90% of large companies use ATS to filter resumes before they reach human recruiters.

ATS systems parse your resume content and compare it against job requirements. Resumes that don't meet certain criteria may never be seen by hiring managers, making ATS optimization crucial for job seekers.

How Our Tool Works

Our ATS Resume Checker analyzes your resume across multiple dimensions:

  • Text Extraction: We extract and analyze all text content from your PDF or Word document
  • Section Analysis: We identify key resume sections like contact info, experience, education, and skills
  • Keyword Density: We analyze how well your resume matches industry-specific keywords
  • Readability: We assess the readability and clarity of your content
  • Formatting Issues: We detect potential parsing problems like tables or special characters

Scoring System

Our scoring algorithm evaluates your resume on a 100-point scale:

  • Section Completeness (40 points): Contact info, experience, education, skills
  • Keyword Relevance (25 points): Industry-specific terms and skills
  • Readability (20 points): Clear, professional language
  • Technical Format (15 points): ATS-friendly formatting
